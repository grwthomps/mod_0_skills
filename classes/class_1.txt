Class: Department

Attributes:

department_name (string)
location (string)
number_of_faculty (integer)
number_of_students (integer)
student_gpa (array)
department_head (string)

Methods:

change_department_head (replaces department_head string with name of new department head)
calculate_average_gpa (calculates average student gpa: sum of student_gpa / number_of_students)
new_faculty_member (increases number_of_faculty by 1)
student_dropout (decreases number_of_students by 1)
