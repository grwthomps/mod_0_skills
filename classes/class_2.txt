Class: Parking Lot

Attributes:

total_number_of_spots (integer)
number_of_empty_spots (integer)
lot_managers (array)
lot_color (string)

Methods:

calculate_spots_filled (calculates number of spots filled by subtracting number_of_empty_spots from total_number_of_spots)
change_lot_color (changes lot_color string)
add_lot_manager (adds string to lot_managers array)
lot_expansion (increases total_number_of_spots by 10)
